About GatherX

Connecting people through seamless virtual event experiences

Our Story

How we're revolutionizing virtual event management

From Vision to Reality

GatherX emerged from a simple observation: virtual events were becoming increasingly important, but the available platforms were fragmented and difficult to use. Event organizers struggled with multiple tools, attendees faced confusing interfaces

With backgrounds in event management and technology, set out to create a unified platform that would serve all stakeholders in the event ecosystem. We believed that virtual events should be as engaging and productive as in-person gatherings, if not more.

Today, GatherX has evolved into a comprehensive solution trusted by thousands of organizations worldwide. We continue to innovate, adding new features and capabilities to make virtual events more accessible, engaging, and valuable for everyone involved.

Our Mission & Vision

What drives us forward every day

Our Mission

To democratize access to high-quality virtual events by providing an intuitive, all-in-one platform that empowers organizers, engages attendees, supports vendors, and delivers value to sponsors.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading virtual event platform, breaking down geographical barriers and creating meaningful connections that transcend physical limitations.
